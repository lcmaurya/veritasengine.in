Institutional Proposal

AI-based Risk Intelligence Engine for Government & Regulated Institutions

Problem Statement

  • Rapid growth of digital fraud, scams, and phishing attacks
  • Fragmented risk signals across platforms and institutions
  • Delayed detection and reactive enforcement

Proposed Solution

Veritas Engine provides a centralized intelligence layer that analyzes behavioral and transactional risk signals in real time and delivers actionable risk indicators to authorized institutions.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-time fraud & scam risk classification
  • Behavioral anomaly detection
  • Secure intelligence sharing via APIs
  • Institution-controlled deployment

Value for Government & Banks

  • Early warning system for emerging fraud patterns
  • Improved inter-agency and inter-bank intelligence coordination
  • Reduction in financial losses and investigation time
  • Supports evidence-based decision making

Deployment Model

  • On-premise or private cloud deployment
  • No public exposure or consumer access
  • Fully auditable and policy-controlled
Engagement Model
• Pilot Phase with selected institutions
• Controlled evaluation and customization
• Formal MoU / Agreement prior to scale deployment

Next Steps

  • Technical and legal review
  • Pilot scope definition
  • Deployment planning

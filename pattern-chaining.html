Pattern-Chaining Intelligence

Pattern-chaining is a behavioral intelligence technique used to identify linked financial activities across wallets, accounts, platforms, and time-windows. The system focuses on relationships and flows, not identities.

   - Amount Structuring

            |
            v

What the Engine Detects

  • Multi-hop transaction chains
  • Layering & structuring indicators
  • Velocity anomalies across wallets
  • Cross-platform fund movement
  • Confidence-weighted risk signals

What the Engine Does NOT Do

  • No wallet ownership attribution
  • No identity resolution
  • No enforcement or blocking actions
  • No public or consumer-level monitoring
Important:
Veritas Engine generates pattern intelligence, not surveillance. All investigative decisions, attribution, and actions remain fully under institutional and legal authority.

Roadmap & Future Capabilities

Strategic evolution plan for Veritas Engine

Phase 1 – Foundation (Current)

  • Core fraud, scam & phishing pattern detection
  • Risk classification (Low / Medium / High)
  • Secure API-based intelligence output
  • Institution-only pilot deployments

Phase 2 – Advanced Intelligence

  • Cross-platform behavioral correlation
  • Temporal risk scoring & trend analysis
  • Adaptive risk confidence levels
  • Enhanced audit & compliance reporting

Phase 3 – Ecosystem Integration

  • Inter-bank and inter-agency intelligence sharing
  • Custom policy-driven risk rules per institution
  • Case-management and investigation support
  • Integration with national cyber & fraud systems

Phase 4 – Strategic Capabilities

  • Early-warning national fraud indicators
  • Sector-wide anonymized risk insights
  • Support for regulatory analytics & supervision
  • Controlled AI-assisted investigation tools

Guiding Principles

  • Privacy-first and regulation-aligned
  • No consumer profiling or surveillance
  • Human-in-the-loop decision support
  • Institutional control at every layer

FAQ – Regulators & Banks

Is Veritas Engine a consumer product?

No. It is strictly an institutional intelligence system.

Does it store user data?

No. No personal or raw transactional data is stored.

Is it an enforcement system?

No. It provides risk intelligence only. Enforcement decisions remain external.

How is integration done?

Via secured APIs or on-premise deployment under institutional control.

Is it compliant with regulatory requirements?

Yes. The system is designed to align with regulatory, audit, and privacy standards.

Who can request access?

Government bodies, banks, regulators, and approved institutions only.

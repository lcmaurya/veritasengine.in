FAQ – Regulators & Financial Institutions

Is Veritas Engine a consumer or retail product?

No. It is strictly an institutional intelligence system.

Does the system store user or wallet data?

No personal data or raw transactions are stored.

Does it identify criminals or illegal activity?

No. It provides probabilistic risk indicators only.

Is this an enforcement or blocking system?

No. All enforcement decisions remain with the institution.

How is it deployed?

On-premise, private cloud, or controlled API integration.

Who can request access?

Government bodies, banks, regulators, and approved institutions only.

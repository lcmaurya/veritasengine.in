Veritas Guard is a free public safety tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we do and do not collect.

📋 WHAT WE COLLECT

Veritas Guard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

When you submit a message for analysis:

· Message text is sent to the Veritas Engine for analysis only
· Analysis result is returned immediately
· Message content is NOT stored on any server
· No user account or identity is required

Usage statistics (scan count, flagged count) are stored locally on your device only using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

🚫 WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect:

· Your name, email, or phone number
· Your location or IP address
· Your device information
· Your browsing history
· Your message content (not stored)
· Any cookies for tracking
· Advertising identifiers
· User profiling data
· Social media trackers
Personal Data Statement

Veritas Guard does not collect or store personal user data.

This includes name, phone number, email address, device identifiers, location data, or browsing history.

The tool operates without user accounts and does not require any personal identification.
⚙️ HOW ANALYSIS WORKS

When you click "Analyze Risk Signals", the text you entered is sent to the Veritas Engine — a deterministic rules-based system hosted at veritasengine.in.

The engine applies fixed, frozen rules to detect risk patterns. It does not use AI, machine learning, or any form of user profiling.

The result is returned to your screen only. The message is not logged, stored, or used for any other purpose.

Data Processing

When a user submits text for analysis, the message content may be temporarily processed by the Veritas Engine service hosted at veritasengine.in.

The message is analyzed using deterministic rule-based detection to identify scam risk signals.

Message content is not permanently stored after analysis and is not associated with any user identity.
📷 IMAGE / OCR FEATURE

If you use the screenshot upload feature, image processing (OCR) runs entirely in your browser using Tesseract.js.

Your image is never uploaded to any server. All text extraction happens locally on your device.

✅ Images never leave your device
🔗 THIRD PARTY SERVICES

Veritas Guard uses the following third-party resources for display only:

· Google Fonts — for typography only
· Tesseract.js CDN — for OCR (runs locally)
· No advertising networks
· No analytics services
· No social media trackers
👶 CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Veritas Guard does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, this tool is safe for general use.

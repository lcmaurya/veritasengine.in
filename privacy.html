Privacy Policy
Effective Date: April 14, 2026

Veritas ("the App") is developed and operated by Molu Corporation. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our practices.

🔒 Our Core Principle: Veritas follows a privacy-first, zero-storage philosophy. We never store your original messages, documents, or personal information on our servers.

1. Data We Collect

Veritas is designed with a zero-storage philosophy. We collect minimal data necessary for the App to function:

  • Message Text: When you scan a message for threat analysis, the text is processed by our AI engine in real-time. Message content is not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Document Hashes: When you register a document, only the cryptographic hash (SHA-256) is stored — never the original document, image, or any personal information contained within it.
  • Timestamps: The date and time of each scan or registration is recorded for verification purposes.
  • Verification Decision: Whether a scan was classified as safe, cautionary, or high-risk (numeric code only, no content).

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Login credentials or passwords
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Original documents, photos, or images
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS content beyond what you manually paste

3. Camera & Photo Library

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely for the purpose of scanning documents for verification. Images are processed entirely on your device to generate a cryptographic hash. The original image is never uploaded to or stored on our servers.

4. Local History

The App maintains a local scan history on your device (using your device's secure storage). This history is:

  • Stored only on your device — never transmitted to us
  • Deletable anytime via the "Clear History" option
  • Automatically removed when you uninstall the App
  • Not accessible by third parties or advertisers

5. How We Use Data

The limited data we process is used exclusively to:

  • Analyze messages for potential fraud or scam indicators
  • Generate and store cryptographic hashes for document verification
  • Maintain the integrity of the verification chain
  • Provide digital signatures as proof of registration

6. Data Security

All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Document hashes are secured using SHA-256 cryptographic hashing and RSA-2048 digital signatures. Our verification chain is tamper-proof — any unauthorized modification is automatically detected.

7. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with third parties. No advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers have access to any information processed through the App.

8. No Account Required

Veritas does not require user registration, login, or any form of account creation. The App functions without collecting any personal identification information.

9. AI-Generated Results Disclaimer

All threat analysis results, trust scores, and document assessments are generated by an automated AI engine. These results are advisory in nature and should not be treated as legal judgments or professional advice. Users should independently verify information before taking action. The developers assume no liability for decisions made based on the App's output.

10. Data Retention

Cryptographic hashes stored on the verification chain are retained indefinitely to ensure ongoing verification capability. No other data is retained beyond the duration of a single analysis session. No personal information is ever retained.

11. Children's Privacy

Veritas is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us for removal.

12. Compliance

Veritas is designed to comply with applicable data protection regulations, including:

  • India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act), 2023
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • Google Play Store Developer Policy requirements

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any modified terms.

14. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, correction) do not apply in the usual sense. However, you retain full control over:

  • Your local scan history (deletable anytime)
  • Your camera and storage permissions (revocable in device settings)
  • Your use of the App (uninstall removes all local data)

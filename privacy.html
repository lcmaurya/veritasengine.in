1. What Veritas Guard does

Veritas Guard helps users check suspicious messages, verify proof records, and create or verify privacy-preserving identity proof records.

Veritas Guard is an advisory safety tool. It does not replace official verification by banks, government offices, police, telecom providers, courts, legal authorities, or identity-issuing agencies.

Veritas Guard एक advisory safety tool है। यह bank, government, police, telecom, legal authority या official identity verification की जगह नहीं लेता।

2. Data processed by the app

Depending on the feature used, Veritas Guard may process the following data:

  • Message text: Text pasted or typed by the user may be sent to the Veritas backend to generate an advisory risk result.
  • Proof codes and hashes: Proof codes, hashes, signatures, or related verification data may be processed to check cryptographic consistency.
  • Identity document image data: If the user chooses Identity Vault features, an identity document image may be processed for proof enrollment or verification.
  • Technical and security data: Server systems may process technical request data needed for security, abuse prevention, debugging, rate limiting, and service operation.

3. Raw KYC document storage

Veritas is designed to store proof commitments, hashes, signatures, masked values, and verification metadata.

Raw KYC documents are not stored by default as proof records.

Veritas default रूप से raw KYC documents को proof records की तरह store करने के लिए design नहीं किया गया है। यह proof commitments, hashes, signatures और verification metadata का उपयोग करता है।

4. Sensitive information warning

Users should not submit or share OTP, PIN, password, CVV, private keys, full bank details, or unnecessary sensitive information.

Veritas Guard will never ask users to share OTP, PIN, password, CVV, private keys, or full bank details for verification.

कृपया OTP, PIN, password, CVV, private keys, full bank details या unnecessary sensitive information share न करें।

5. How data is used

  • To provide scam or fraud advisory analysis
  • To create proof records
  • To verify proof records
  • To verify identity proof consistency
  • To maintain system security and integrity
  • To prevent abuse and troubleshoot service issues

6. Data sharing

Veritas Guard does not sell user data.

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to operate the app and backend service, such as hosting, storage, security, or deployment providers.

7. Data retention

Proof records, hashes, signatures, commitments, masked values, and verification metadata may be retained to support future verification.

Users may contact founder.veritas@gmail.com for privacy-related questions or deletion requests where applicable.

8. Security

Veritas Guard uses cryptographic hashes and signatures for proof verification. Production services should use HTTPS for data transmission.

No digital system can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but Veritas Guard is designed with privacy-first proof handling and limited raw data storage by default.

9. Important limitation

A proof match confirms cryptographic consistency only. It does not guarantee legal authenticity, ownership, truthfulness, or real-world authority of a person, message, or document.

Users should always verify important matters through official or trusted sources.

Proof match केवल cryptographic consistency दिखाता है। यह किसी व्यक्ति, message या document की legal authenticity, ownership, truthfulness या authority की guarantee नहीं देता।

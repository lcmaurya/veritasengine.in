Veritas Engine

AI-based Fraud, Scam & Phishing Intelligence System

Purpose

Veritas Engine is designed to support government bodies, banks, and regulated institutions by providing real-time risk intelligence related to fraud, scams, and malicious digital behavior.

Problem Statement

  • Rapid growth of digital fraud and phishing attacks
  • Delayed detection using traditional rule-based systems
  • High investigation cost and operational load

Proposed Solution

Veritas Engine analyzes behavioral and transactional signals in real time and produces structured risk intelligence for institutional decision-making.

Key Capabilities

  • Risk scoring (Low / Medium / High)
  • Behavioral pattern analysis
  • Phishing & scam indicator detection
  • Secure API-based intelligence delivery

Data Privacy & Security

  • No raw or personal user data is stored
  • Engine core remains private and isolated
  • Only processed intelligence outputs are shared

Deployment Options

  • On-premise deployment
  • Private cloud / Hybrid model
  • API-only integration

Intended Users

  • Government & Regulatory Bodies
  • Banks & Financial Institutions
  • FinTech & Digital Payment Platforms
  • Cyber Crime & Intelligence Units

Status

Pilot Phase – Available for institutional evaluation and controlled integration.
